# RL for E-comm Inventory Management

by dr.Karthik
Reinforcement Learning (RL) can be applied to e-commerce inventory management by modeling the inventory system as a Markov Decision Process (MDP). The agent (RL algorithm) observes the current inventory levels, demand forecasts, and other relevant factors (state), and then decides on actions such as ordering new stock, adjusting prices, or initiating promotions. The reward function would typically be designed to maximize profit, minimize holding costs, and avoid stockouts, thereby optimizing the overall inventory strategy.
